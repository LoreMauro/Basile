Welcome to Basile online!

Since the beginning of time, fables have been a staple part of everybody's life.

They are the way we learn how to act, how to conduct ourselves and how to avoid the many dangers that we could encounter during our life's journey.

Giambattista Basile has been the first to codified many of our beloved stories (like Cinderella, Cat in Boots and Snow White). During the centuries though, this incredible stories have been either denautared or completely forgotten, and many of the core characteristics of these immortal characters have become completely unrecognized.

With the complicitness of the great prejudices the critics have always had with dialectal traditions, Basile's works have slowly faded in the background of history.

The time has come to recuperate them!

How did a man like Basile see the world? And how did he structure this world?

Let's find out!
