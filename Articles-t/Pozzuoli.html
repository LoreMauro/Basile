Pozzuoli

Second Day, The Dove

Pozzuoli (Italian pronunciation: [potˈtswɔːli]; Latin: Puteoli ; Neapolitan: Pezzulo) is a city and comune of the Metropolitan City of Naples, in the Italian region of Campania. It is the main city of the Phlegrean Peninsula.
